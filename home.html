Welcome to Super Cool Cars!

What we do

We drop updates about car productions, recent car releases and car gists. Also, we are known for our useful car driving and maintenance tips.
In addition to that, we host weekly car shows. It's a platform for youngsters who are interested in becoming F1 drivers to showcase their talents to the world while also catching the attention of the car racing industry.

What inspires what we do

We are a huge fan of talents and skills and watching younsters' talents and skills go to waste makes us uncomfortable. So we got the inspiration of creating a platform where we can talk about cars and also showcase all these young talents and skills to the world.

What we hope to achieve

We hope that we would be able to showcase these talents and skills to the world in time and also secure our youngsters' lifelong dreams by doing so.
